CHOSEN DISH




Kaldereta

Kaldereta is a hearty Filipino stew made with tender chunks of meat (often beef or goat) cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce. The meat is typically marinated and then stewed with vegetables such as potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers, along with ingredients like garlic, onions, and liver spread for added depth of flavor. Kaldereta is known for its savory and slightly tangy taste, often served with rice and enjoyed during special occasions and gatherings in Filipino households.

Kaldereta Preparation

Preparation time:
10 minutes

Cooking time:
1 hour

Total time:
1 hour 10 minutes

Number of servings:
6

Ingredients:

  • • 2 lbs beef, cubed.
  • • 3 pieces garlic cloves, crushed and chopped.
  • • 1 piece onion, finely chopped.
  • • 2 cups beef broth.
  • • 1 piece red bell pepper, sliced.
  • • 1 piece green bell pepper, sliced.
  • • 1 cup tomato sauce.
  • • ½ cup liver spread, processed using blender.
  • • 1 teaspoon chili flakes.
  • • 3 pieces dried bay leaves.
  • • 2 cups potatoes, sliced.
  • • 2 cups carrots, sliced.
  • • 1/4 cup cooking oil.
  • • ⅔ cup green olives.
  • • Salt and pepper to taste.

Directions

Step 1

Heat the oil

Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker. Sauté the onion and garlic. Add the beef. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown.

Step 2

Stir it

Add the dried bay leaves and crushed pepper. Stir.Add the liver spread. Stir. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth. Cook the beef until it becomes tender

Step 3

Add the other ingredients

Add potato and carrots. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot. Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more.

Step 4

Serve and Enjoy!

Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve Hot. Enjoy!
